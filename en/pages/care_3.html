What Is IBPS Booster Peel?

It improves on the stinging and other drawbacks of conventional peels, making it a peel gentle enough even for sensitive skin.

Next-generation, irritation-free peeling ingredients leave the skin's lipid barrier intact while gently lifting away built-up dead skin, strengthening the skin barrier for healthier skin.

Bacteriophage Prevents breakouts and boosts the skin's immunity.

It is gaining attention as an alternative for treating antibiotic-resistant bacteria.

LHA Aids exfoliation and sebum control, removing dead skin gently with minimal irritation.

IBPS Booster Peel Treatment Method
STEP 01

IBPS Booster Peel is applied to the entire face or targeted trouble areas.

STEP 02 With no special equipment—application alone— the active ingredients absorb deep into the skin, and after about 5 minutes, the remaining solution is gently wiped away.

STEP 03 When combined with laser treatment, the results can be maximized.

IBPS Booster Peel Q&A
Will my skin flake after treatment?

Unlike conventional peels, it causes little flaking, so daily life is barely affected.

How often should I get the treatment?

Consistent sessions every 2–4 weeks are most effective.
