What is EGF Booster?

EGF Booster is a fibroblast culture medium containing FGF and various growth factors, effective for hydration, antioxidant protection, anti-aging, skin regeneration, and wrinkle improvement.

Ingredients such as collagen, elastin, and hyaluronic acid promote cell production, strengthening skin regeneration and the skin barrier to support anti-aging.

It is applied via a 0.25mm MTS (microneedle) and delivered into the skin, creating hundreds of microchannels to deliver active ingredients to deep layers of the skin, and increases absorption through LDM's ultrasonic phoresis mode.

Firmness
Contains FGF that supports fibroblast growth

A 150,000 PPM fibroblast culture medium supports the formation of elastin and collagen in the skin, creating a firmness-improving effect.

Brightening The antioxidant power of three plant stem cells With antioxidant power twice that of vitamin C, it protects the skin from UV rays and supports skin regeneration.

Unlike adipose stem cell culture medium, fibroblast culture medium

harvests cells from skin tissue rather than fat.

It is not an ordinary stem cell culture medium that merely produces collagen; it breaks down aged collagen fibers and fills the dermis with new collagen, creating firm, resilient skin.
