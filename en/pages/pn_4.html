What is Caviar Rebuilder?
PDRN extracted from the DNA of sturgeon and salmon,

combined with plant-derived exosomes, effectively boosts the skin's regenerative power, supporting anti-aging and brightening.

Sturgeon & Salmon PDRN Skin regeneration, strengthening, and inflammation care Hyaluronic Acid Delivers moisture to relieve dryness and prevent aging Ginseng & Broccoli Exosomes Collagen regeneration and vitamin benefits revitalize the skin Botanically Extracted Brightening Agents Improves skin tone and delivers a powerful brightening effect

STEP 1

Foundational Skin Rebuilding
Skin regeneration & brightening

Caviar Rebuilder is built around sturgeon PDRN and salmon PDRN as its core ingredients, working together with exosomes extracted from ginseng and broccoli to regenerate the skin healthily while delivering a powerful brightening effect.

Through the synergy of stem cell culture medium and wild ginseng exosomes, it lays the foundation for healthy skin and delivers instant firmness, regeneration, and brightening.

Because it aims for fundamental skin improvement rather than a mere temporary effect, as treatment sessions accumulate, the effects of Caviar Rebuilder last progressively longer.

Sturgeon PDRN & Salmon PDRN → Penetrate deep into the skin for cell regeneration and recovery Ginseng Exosomes → Revitalize the skin and supply nutrients Natural Brightening Extracts → Improve skin tone and brighten

STEP 2

The fusion of Er:YAG laser and key active ingredients
Fundamental improvement of the papillary dermis

Using the Er:YAG laser, this stage intensively regenerates the papillary dermis.

The papillary dermis is the key layer connecting the dermis and epidermis; as aging progresses, it thins and weakens, leading to concerns such as fine lines and enlarged pores.

Regenerating the papillary dermis targets fundamental skin improvement rather than temporary lifting, allowing you to see lasting change.

Restored dermal thickness → The laser delivers active ingredients deep into the skin, regenerating and thickening the papillary dermis.

Firmness and lifting effect → Through collagen regeneration and hyaluronic acid activation, it strengthens skin firmness and maximizes lifting.

Relief from inner tightness → Dryness is reduced, delivering ample moisture that hydrates from within.
