What is Fotona QX-MAX Dual Toning?

The Fotona QX-MAX destroys pigment cells deep within the skin while inducing collagen production—a laser device effective for improving overall skin elasticity. With higher output than conventional lasers, it delivers even energy across the entire skin, maximizing results.
