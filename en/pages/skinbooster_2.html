What is Jou Bloom Booster?

Improving wrinkles around the lips, eyes, and forehead, restoring volume in the cheeks, and face contouring—this is a polylactide skin booster.

Based on polylactide, a natural compound that promotes the synthesis of new collagen molecules, it not only fills wrinkles but also restructures cellular processes delivering a dual effect that increases skin firmness and tension.
