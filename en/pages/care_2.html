LaLa Peel

A gentle PLLHA-based peel that improves clarity and texture from deep within
LaLa Peel LaLa Peel is built around LHA (Lipo Hydroxy Acid), the gold standard of 4th-generation, mildly alkaline 'replenishing peels.' It works gently on the stratum corneum, selectively dissolving only proteins while preserving the skin barrier, and aids absorption of active ingredients to improve elasticity, tone, texture, sebum balance, and overall skin condition. What Is LaLa Peel?
Without irritating the skin, LaLa Peel

gently refines built-up dead skin, excess sebum, and pores while stimulating collagen production— a 'zero-irritation, zero-downtime' peel program that restores the skin's natural strength.

LHA, HP‑SoI™, and plant-based lipids work in concert to induce natural exfoliation and cell renewal at once, comprehensively caring for acne, pigmentation, loss of elasticity, and more.

LaLa Peel Functions & Benefits
Refined Facial Contours

By clearing dead skin and replenishing moisture and collagen, skin texture becomes smooth and refined.

Better Texture & Tone The skin's surface is evenly refined, and a dull complexion turns clear and bright.

Natural Lifting While strengthening the skin barrier, it induces collagen remodeling.

Sebum & Pore Care Excess sebum, acne, enlarged pores, and more are cared for without irritation.

Who LaLa Peel Is For
Those whose facial texture or tone

is dull and uneven In need of an irritation-free peel: sensitive skin types Troubled by acne and sebum: oily skin types Those with sagging pores and reduced elasticity Those needing ongoing care for recurring flaking and breakouts With zero downtime, those wanting a speedy recovery

Treatment Method & Schedule
Treatment Method

A mildly alkaline peeling solution containing LHA is applied to the skin and absorbed, removing dead skin and sebum while delivering active ingredients.

Treatment Interval We recommend 3–5 sessions at 1–2 week intervals, adjustable to suit your skin's condition.
